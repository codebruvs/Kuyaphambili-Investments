About Kuyaphambili Investments

As a black-owned and managed company in South Africa, Kuyaphambili Investments takes a 360-degree view of IT investments for government and corporate businesses through innovative technology development.

We understand that the most critical issues in today's business are process-centric. The pressures of commoditization and globalization demand optimized processes to reduce costs, maintain margins, innovate, and improve customer relations.

At the core of delivering services are processes that must be well-defined, fine-tuned, and optimized. We leverage consulting services and innovative technology, especially in software development, to continuously improve these processes and deliver the best outcomes for customers and citizens.
